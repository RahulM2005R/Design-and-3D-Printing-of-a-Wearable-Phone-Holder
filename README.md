# Design and 3D Printing of a Wearable Phone Holder

A lightweight, ergonomic, and customizable wearable smartphone holder designed using **SolidWorks** and fabricated using **Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)** 3D printing technology with **PLA filament**.

The device is designed to be worn on the user's forearm, providing hands-free smartphone access during activities such as online learning, gaming, content creation, workshops, and multitasking. The project demonstrates the capabilities of additive manufacturing in developing innovative, low-cost, and user-friendly consumer products.
